# 🎯 Identifying Targets in Bug Bounty Programs

---

## 🔍 Bug Bounty Programs  

Bug bounty programs are initiatives where organizations reward individuals for discovering and reporting vulnerabilities in their systems and applications. These programs can be run by individual companies, governments, or third-party platforms.

---

## ⭐ Importance  

Understanding how to effectively target these programs is crucial for ethical hackers. Proper targeting ensures that you allocate your resources and time efficiently, maximizing your chances of discovering vulnerabilities and earning rewards.

---

## 🎲 Targeting Strategy

### 1️⃣ Research  
Conduct thorough research on the company and its products. Use tools like Shodan, DNS interrogation, and other reconnaissance tools to gather useful information.

### 2️⃣ Analyzing Attack Surface  
Identify the attack surface by understanding the technologies used, third-party services, and existing vulnerabilities.

### 3️⃣ Prioritizing Targets  
Evaluate target systems based on their potential impact. Focus on the systems that handle sensitive data or are critical to business operations.

---

## 📋 Scope  

Every bug bounty program has specific rules and scopes defining what is allowed and what is not. Carefully read the guidelines to avoid disqualification or legal issues.
